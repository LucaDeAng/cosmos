### Annual Budget Process Timeline

| Month | Activity |
|-------|----------|
| July-August | Strategic planning inputs, demand gathering |
| September | Business case development, prioritization |
| October | Draft budget compilation |
| November | Executive review and approval |
| December | Final adjustments, communication |
| January | Execution begins |

### Budget Variance Management

| Variance | Action |
|----------|--------|
| <5% | Normal tolerance, monitor |
| 5-10% | Management review, justify |
| 10-15% | Executive escalation, remediation plan |
| >15% | Board-level review, significant intervention |

## Best Practices

1. **Benchmark regularly** - Compare against industry peers annually
2. **Track Run/Grow/Transform** - Monitor ratio shifts over time
3. **Measure value, not just cost** - ROI and business outcomes matter
4. **Plan for flexibility** - 10-15% reserve for opportunities
5. **Invest in optimization** - 3-5% of budget for continuous improvement
6. **Balance short and long term** - Don't sacrifice transformation for operations
