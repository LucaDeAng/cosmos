# Catalog Prioritization Knowledge Base

## 1. Scoring Frameworks

### WSJF
WSJF = (BusinessValue + TimeCriticality + RiskReduction) / JobSize
- ≥4.0: Critical | 3.0-3.9: High | 2.0-2.9: Medium | <2.0: Low

### ICE
ICE = Impact × Confidence × Ease
- ≥500: QuickWin | 300-499: Good | 100-299: Moderate | <100: Low

### Kano
- MustBe: Security, Compliance
- OneDimensional: Performance
- Attractive: AI, Innovation

## 2. Quadrants

| Quadrant | Value | Effort | Actions |
|----------|-------|--------|---------|
| QuickWin | ≥60 | <50 | Accelerate |
| Strategic | ≥60 | ≥50 | Invest |
| FillIn | <60 | <50 | Maintain |
| Rationalize | <60 | ≥50 | Sunset |

## 3. Action Rules

- QuickWin + MUST → Accelerate (Immediate)
- Strategic + MUST → Invest (Short-term)
- FillIn + Retention≥0.5 → Maintain
- Rationalize → Sunset/Migrate
