# Industry Verticals Catalog

- category: catalog_industries
- source: internal

Catalogo dei settori industriali per classificazione portfolio items.

## Manufacturing Industries

### Automotive
- **OEM**: Original Equipment Manufacturers
- **Tier 1 Suppliers**: Fornitori diretti componentistica
- **Tier 2/3 Suppliers**: Subfornitori, componentisti
- **Aftermarket**: Ricambi, accessori, tuning
- **Fleet Management**: Gestione flotte aziendali

### Aerospace & Defense
- **Civil Aviation**: Aeromobili commerciali, elicotteri
- **Military**: Difesa, sistemi d'arma, veicoli militari
- **Space**: Satelliti, lanciatori, sistemi spaziali
- **MRO**: Maintenance, Repair, Overhaul

### Chemicals & Pharma
- **Basic Chemicals**: Chimica di base, petrolchimica
- **Specialty Chemicals**: Chimica fine, additivi
- **Pharmaceuticals**: Farmaci, API, biosimilari
- **Agrochemicals**: Fertilizzanti, fitofarmaci

### Food & Beverage
- **Food Processing**: Trasformazione alimentare
- **Beverage Production**: Produzione bevande
- **Packaging**: Confezionamento, imbottigliamento
- **Cold Chain**: Logistica refrigerata

### Textile & Fashion
- **Fiber Production**: Produzione fibre
- **Fabric Manufacturing**: Tessitura, tintura
- **Apparel**: Abbigliamento, accessori
- **Technical Textiles**: Tessuti tecnici, industriali

## Service Industries

### Healthcare
- **Hospitals**: Ospedali, cliniche, ambulatori
- **Diagnostics**: Laboratori, imaging, screening
- **Home Care**: Assistenza domiciliare
- **Pharma Distribution**: Distribuzione farmaceutica

### Financial Services
- **Banking**: Retail, corporate, investment banking
- **Insurance**: Vita, danni, riassicurazione
- **Asset Management**: Gestione patrimoniale
- **Fintech**: Pagamenti digitali, lending, blockchain

### Retail & Distribution
- **Brick & Mortar**: Negozi fisici, catene
- **E-commerce**: Vendite online, marketplace
- **Wholesale**: Grossisti, distributori
- **Logistics**: 3PL, last mile, warehousing

### Professional Services
- **Consulting**: Management, strategy, technology
- **Legal**: Studi legali, compliance
- **Accounting**: Revisione, fiscale, consulenza
- **Engineering**: Progettazione, R&D

## Technology Industries

### Software & IT
- **Enterprise Software**: ERP, CRM, SCM vendors
- **SaaS**: Cloud applications, platforms
- **System Integration**: Integratori, implementatori
- **Managed Services**: MSP, outsourcing IT

### Telecommunications
- **Mobile Operators**: Operatori mobili
- **Fixed Line**: Telefonia fissa, fibra
- **ISP**: Internet service providers
- **Infrastructure**: Torri, data center, cavi

### Electronics
- **Semiconductors**: Chip, wafer, foundry
- **Consumer Electronics**: TV, audio, gaming
- **Industrial Electronics**: Automazione, controllo
- **Components**: Passivi, connettori, PCB

## Energy & Utilities

### Energy Production
- **Oil & Gas**: Upstream, midstream, downstream
- **Renewable**: Solar, wind, hydro, geothermal
- **Nuclear**: Centrali nucleari, combustibile
- **Power Generation**: Centrali termoelettriche

### Utilities
- **Electricity**: Distribuzione elettrica, smart grid
- **Gas Distribution**: Rete gas, GNL
- **Water**: Acquedotti, depurazione
- **Waste Management**: Rifiuti, riciclo

## Construction & Real Estate

### Construction
- **Residential**: Edilizia residenziale
- **Commercial**: Uffici, retail, hospitality
- **Infrastructure**: Strade, ponti, ferrovie
- **Industrial**: Stabilimenti, magazzini

### Real Estate
- **Development**: Sviluppo immobiliare
- **Property Management**: Gestione immobili
- **REIT**: Fondi immobiliari
- **Facility Services**: Servizi agli immobili
